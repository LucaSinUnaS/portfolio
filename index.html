Projects

¿Qué Hay Hoy?

Winning project of the tenth edition of the Ceibal Robotics, Programming and Video Games Olympiad.

Users can publish events (which must first be validated by an administrator), write comments on each event, and search with a large number of filters for any event they want.

MontevideoAPI

Site that connects to the Montevideo API and shows all the bus stops of the STM. When you click on a stop, it shows which lines pass through it, the next ten buses to pass, and the actual location of the available buses.

Bestia en Escape

Endless runner for PC and Android where your goal is to hold out as long as possible dodging spikes and hitting enemy bullets.

Daygizer

App that helps you to control your wellbeing day by day. It has several functions, such as a to-do list, a list where you can write down your energy status, and another one where you can write down your rest hours.

GD Song Verifier

Chrome and Firefox extension that retrieves the IDs of Newgrounds songs, and checks if they can be used inside the popular video game Geometry Dash. They connect to a server created by myself, hosted separately, which, in turn, connects to the game's servers to retrieve the necessary information.

¡Adivina el país!

Short game where the outline of a South American country slowly appears, and your goal is to guess which country it belongs to. By correctly guessing all of them, you win the game.
